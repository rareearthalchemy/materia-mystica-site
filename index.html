What is Materia Mystica?

"Alchemy is the Art of Nature. It reveals the secret virtue in all things, awakening their highest expression."

Materia Mystica is the living archive and creative vessel of alchemist and healer Nick Grossenbacher, founder of Rare Earth Alchemy and Reålea Skincare. It exists to illuminate the esoteric dimensions of healing, matter, and consciousness—through writings, remedies, and sacred craft.

This is a return to the philosopher's path—where nature is not a resource to exploit, but a sacred text to read, honor, and transmute.

Ens Scorpionis

The Scorpion Opens the Door.

In the untouched oak-studded hills of Mendocino County, the scorpion dwells in stillness—an emissary of transformation, protection, and the deeper currents of nature’s intelligence.

Ens Scorpionis is a rare alchemical tincture crafted from wild-caught scorpions, prepared according to the protocols of the Primum Ens—a high-level spagyric method known to concentrate the spiritual essence of a substance.

This tincture is not for the faint-hearted. It is a shamanic key to initiate deep psychic cleansing, protection, and personal rebirth.
